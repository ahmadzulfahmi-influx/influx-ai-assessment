**Welcome to Your Assessment**

Thank you for your interest in joining our team!

We want to support you through this stage and help you do your best. Therefore, before starting the test, please take a few minutes to read the information below carefully.

**What to expect**

You’ll be chatting with an interviewer about your past experiences. There are **5 main questions**, and the assessment usually takes around **60 minutes**.

You have enough time to think, type, and share your answers properly, so there’s no need to rush.

**How to answer**

Please answer using your **own voice and your own words**.

We really appreciate it if you don’t use AI to polish or rewrite your answers. After all, what we want is to get to know **you** better — your experience, your thinking, your challenges, and how you handled real situations.

When answering, try to share specific examples. Tell us what happened, what you did, and what the result was. ***Detailed and honest answers help us understand you better***.

If you can’t think of a work example, you can also use examples from school, personal projects, volunteer work, or other life experiences.

Take your time, be honest, and share your experience as naturally as you can. We wish you all the best!

**Estimated time: ~60 minutes**
